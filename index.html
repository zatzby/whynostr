Why Nostr?

Because it is

Nostr is an emerging network built on a decentralized protocol, where anyone can easily join and immediately begin interacting with a global community of individuals.

With a few simple clicks, you generate a public and private key - think of it like a username and password - that allow you to access a growing ecosystem of applications.

With Nostr, the power is returned to the individual. Your journey is not controlled by algorithms, riddled with unnecessary and irrelevant ads, but is up to YOU. Learn more about the protocol and join the fun!
